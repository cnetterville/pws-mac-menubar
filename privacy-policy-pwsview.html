Privacy Policy for PWSView

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how "PWSView" (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

"PWSView" is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from you. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or any other contact information.
  • Your location data (GPS, IP address, etc.).
  • Any data from your Ecowitt weather station. All communication with your Ecowitt device or services is handled locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Usage data, analytics, or crash reports.
  • Device identifiers.

2. How Your Data is Handled (Locally on Your Device)

The App connects directly to the Ecowitt.net service based on the configuration you provide within the App. All data retrieved from Ecowitt.net (such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, etc.) is processed and displayed entirely on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or any third parties by the App.

3. No Third-Party Access to Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal or usage data, there is no information for us to share with third parties.

4. Data Security

As we do not collect or store any of your data, there is no data to secure on our servers. Your Ecowitt weather data remains on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As such, we are in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
